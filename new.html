What To Expect

Welcome to Glory Temple which is a ministry of ICGC . You are sure to find Christ-Centered atmosphere that will aid you to grow healthily in your walk with God. The language of the service is predominantly English and French. Our services are intentionally designed to warmly welcome both our international friends and families visiting Ghana, as well as Ghanaians who prefer to worship in English. It’s an opportunity to connect with people from diverse backgrounds and locations, coming together to serve and grow in their knowledge of the Father.

If you haven't yet begun a relationship with the Father or are a new believer, this is the perfect place for you. We’d love to connect with you, share fellowship, and support your Christian journey in any way we can.

What is a family?

The Family system in the Church is designed to encourage Bible study and fellowship among members who live in the same area. While the main church oversees the meeting schedule and activities of the Community group, each group is led by a family leader, who may also serve as the Bible study facilitator. This leader ensures the smooth organization of group activities and provides reports on their progress.
In addition to Bible study, cell group activities may include prayer. Meetings are held at a location that suits all members, such as members' homes or the church premises, depending on what’s most convenient. The meeting times are also agreed upon by the group members.

Why You Should Join!

If you're seeking additional spiritual nourishment beyond what’s provided during the main services , the Family is perfect for you! If you're looking for a close-knit community within the larger church, the Family (Community) group provides that too. The home cell offers a more personal connection with fellow members and helps foster spiritual growth. If you’re not yet part of a family group, don’t hesitate to fill out the form below. You won’t regret it!
