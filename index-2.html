Welcome to ICGC

We are a vibrant and loving community under the Church of ICGC. We are very delighted to have you join us today. Whether you are a long-time member or a first-time visitor, we want you to feel at home and be embraced by the love of Christ.
Join us for our dynamic Worship services, enriching Bible studies, and various Fellowship activities designed to build up your intimacy with God and not negleting fellowship with one another. As a church, we encourage active participation, through our God-giving talents and gifts share your talents and gifts.
May your time with us be filled with God's presence, joy, and blessings. We look forward to worshipping and growing in the Faith together and abounding in all goods works. In Christ's Love

2025 THEME OF ICGC

This theme emphasizes the importance of centering one's life on Christ, following His teachings, and experiencing His transformative power. It highlightes the need for Christians to walk in the ways of Jesus and rely on Him in all aspects of their lives1. The theme encourages congregants to deepen their faith, seek spiritual renewal, and embrace the promise of a new beginning with Jesus at the core of their journey. It's a call to live a life that reflects the love, grace, and teachings of Jesus Christ.
