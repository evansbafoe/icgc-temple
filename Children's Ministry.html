Children Ministry

The ICGC Glory Temple children ministry takes care of your children while you enjoy service. We take in children between ages 1 to 12. Be rest assured that your children are safe with us while you enjoy your time with the Lord.
The ministry is well resourced with items as well as teachers to teach and train your children in the ways of Lord as well as take care of them while service is on going. Our meeting grounds has been baby proofed to prevent injury during service and we have enough teachers to keep keen eyes on your children while service goes on. Remember, train up the child the way he should go and when he grows he would not depart from it; we are here to facilitate the process.
