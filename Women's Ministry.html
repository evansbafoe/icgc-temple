Women Ministry

Slogan:


Holiness - Unto the Lord | Let Woman be Silent - And Learn the word in all submission
A virtious Wife - Is the crown of her husband | A Graious Woman - Retains Honour!
The ICGC Glory Temple Women Ministry exists to handle all things relating to being a woman of virtue. It aims at helping our young ladies and mothers recognise who they are in Christ as well as being their best versions in him. The ministry treats all subjects and addresses all challenges that come with being a virtuos woman in a time such as this
The programmes organised by the ministry include a wide variety targetted at addressing diverse aspectss of being a woman ranging from spiritual events such as prayer meetings to issues of social nature such as cooking events as well as socialisation meetings like excurssions et al.
